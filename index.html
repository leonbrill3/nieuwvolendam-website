Rooted in Community, Built on Integrity

Since the 1960s, Nieuw Volendam N.V. has been deeply invested in the heart of our Caribbean communities—from the historic streets of Punda in Curaçao to Front Street in Sint Maarten.

We believe in honoring the architectural heritage that defines our islands while thoughtfully integrating modern design. Our portfolio includes carefully restored historic buildings alongside contemporary developments, creating spaces where businesses thrive and communities flourish. Every property we manage reflects our commitment to preserving the character of Punda and Front Street while meeting the needs of today's businesses and residents.
